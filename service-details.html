Real Estate

Our real estate arm provides a platform for our stakeholders, partners and investors to leverage On our experience and sector knowledge, which aids us in the pursuit of emerging prospects, high-value return opportunities as private equity investors and managers in residential and commercial estate .

Our investment philosophy is to partner with proven local operating expertise to analyze and execute low-risk, high return value real estate investment and projects.

We pay keen attention to emerging opportunities within the local market to invest and partner.

Today, in the residential housing niche, our Company focuses on acquisition of properties for long-term rentals and construction projects involving mid-size to large shopping malls, residential schemes, estates and hospitality facilities.

With our unique contemporary style and total passion for connecting people with property, we aspire to provide the ultimate real estate solutions for todays modern consumer.

We are not confined by traditional real estate boundaries when it comes to buying , selling, renting or asset management, instead we are always finding new and innovative ways to satisfy our clients needs and be at the forefront in the real estate market.

Information Technology

We invest into early-stage technology ideas and companies. This is at the heart of our commitments to igniting local ideas into global successes .
Today, we reach out and connect with budding technology ideas and inventions that require seed financing and startup investment.
We assist entrepreneurs who are aiming to solve big problems in innovative and our firm is built by, and for ideators, founders and innovators – we invest from ideation onwards, because we want to partner with founders, take a product tha works and make it a global success.

New Energy

Our energy arm is positioned to explore diversified possibilities and interest across energy exploration, development and renewable energy.

Globatos Group typically focuses on regions which may have been overlooked and where it can participate as an early entrant. We have a deep infrastructure that will drive the local energy market forward.

As Nigeria continues to desire stead and uninterrupted power supply, there is the need to go beyond the construction of gas power plants and also look into alternative technologies especially renewable energy sources to power rural homes which constitute higher percentage of the Nigerian population.

With this in mind, we partner and invest in collaborations that drive the development of renewable Energy – specifically Solar and Inverter, Backup Systems, Waste to Energy plant, Land- Filled Gas Plants and Wind Energy.

Our approach is hands-on, asset-first and technology orientated. We look to achieve our returns through holding equity in well-conceived and constructed solutions, products and projects that contributes to the growth of the local renewable energy market in Africa.

Embracing solar, wind, battery storage ,concentrated solar power and waste to energy technologies, we are focused on creating and delivering solutions that align with long-term local demand for renewable energy and its opportunities for our investing partners .
